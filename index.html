My UX Skills

User Research

I use user research to understand the needs, wants, and limitations of my target users, in order to create user-centered designs that effectively meet their goals and objectives.

Wire Framing

I use wireframing to visually communicate my ideas for the layout, structure and functionality of a website or application, by creating simplified sketches or digital mockups, allowing for efficient and effective feedback and iteration on the design before moving on to the visual design phase.

User Interviews

I use user interviews as a skill to gain insights and gather qualitative data about the users by asking open-ended questions, observing their behaviors and listening to their feedback, in order to inform and validate design decisions and improve the overall user experience.
